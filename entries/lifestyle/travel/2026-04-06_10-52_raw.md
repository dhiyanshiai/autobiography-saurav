As we entered the city, we found our Hotel, Cinnamon- city of dreams. It was one of the best locations and one of the most expensive hotels in Colombo with a Casino in-built and multiple shopping complex within.

I was awestruck looking at the magnificent construction of buildings present in the city of Colombo, it was pleasant to see Taj, ITC in one-line, upon research I found the building of ITC was constructed by L&T; indeed a proud moment for us, as Indians.

The hotel had a beautiful beech side view and had an exceptional view. 
It was great to be at the location, while everyone checked in and chilled in their rooms, I took a stroll around and explored multiple places in and around the hotel, where I identified one Galle place mall and roamed in to find multiple shopping, dining and entertainment options within.

Post that we went for dinner, in one of the famous places, where authentic srilankan cuisine is served, named Upali's by Nawaloka.
It had multiple options and I enjoyed the crab that was ordered, it was one of the messiest meals I had eaten, with both hands, however, one of the best tastes and fresher seafood. 
We had almost 50-60 Hoppers there, and apparently, the restaurant had to stop us for ordering more, as it got really late.

After sometime, we all decided to go for a stroll around the Marine Drive in Colombo, opposite Taj Samudra, and it was soothing, the blend of sea drawing fresh air and the locals selling different varieties of seafood was good to see, along with that we had an Ice cream - Fantastic chocolate flavor before we left for Hotel.

Once, we came in the Hotel, all of us entered the Casino, which was a new experience for me, and first of its kind.
There were many people playing multiple games like slot machine, poker, black jack, roulette, it was good to see, however, considering my limited interest in casino; I was bored and decided to go back to my room and crash, while others continued playing and then chilling for some more time in the get together.
I was tired due to the walk and lower sleep, I thought it would be wise to get asleep due to the health challenges I faced a couple of days back, and a day more left for the trip. 

Excited for the next day.
